Hello World!

November 18, 2024

For whatever reason, you have come to the conclusion that I need to learn how to write software!

More often than not, your first choice is Which programming language should I learn? After further research, you discover a plethora of online courses, coding bootcamps, and Hello World! tutorials. You start to raise questions: What are the best courses? Should I enroll in classes? Why are bootcamps so expensive?

You become stagnant, overwhelmed by the wealth of information and choice.

In reality, it takes many months, even years, to become a proficient software developer. Training is intensive and expensive for good reason. For people with prior exposure, these are good options. For complete beginners, they are not.

Why?

You have to train your brain to think like a developer!

This requires taking a step back to broadly understand how to fundamentally breakdown an application. Whether you want to build a website or a mobile app, the process is the same.

So before you write your first Hello World!, let’s start rewiring that brain!
